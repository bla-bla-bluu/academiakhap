## Full description (max 4000 characters)
```
Academia Khap is a research-oriented platform focused on evidence-based historical study,
cultural documentation, social analysis, rural heritage preservation, and intellectual
engagement across communities.

RESEARCH ARCHIVE
Read in-depth research articles on rural heritage, zamindari history, Jat history,
architecture, and regional cultural documentation — searchable, organized by category, and
free to read without an account.

ABOUT & COLLABORATION
Learn about the Academia Khap team and explore ways to collaborate on research, documentation,
media, and community initiatives.

MEMBER & ADMIN ACCOUNTS
Academia Khap also supports its internal community of researchers and scholars who receive
funding and scholarships:
- Admins record incoming donations and organizational expenses, with an automatically
  calculated running balance.
- Admins allot funds and scholarships to trustees, members, and scholars, and can create
  accounts with the appropriate role.
- Members, trustees, and scholars can see their own allotted funds, log expenses against them
  with a short description, and track their remaining balance.
- Trustees and members (not scholars) can also see organization-wide donation and expense
  totals for transparency.

COMMUNITY DISCUSSION
Signed-in members can post short text updates (up to 500 characters) and comment on each
other's posts to discuss research, events, and community matters. No images or attachments —
just focused, text-based discussion.

Academia Khap accounts are created by an administrator; this app does not have public
self-registration, since membership is tied to Academia Khap's research and scholarship
program.
```

## Data Safety form (Play Console)
When Play Console asks what data your app collects, declare:
- **Personal info**: Name, Email address — collected, used for account functionality, not
  shared with third parties, not optional (required to use the member features).
- **Financial info**: collected (donation/expense/allocation records), used for app
  functionality only, not shared with third parties.
- **App activity**: User-generated content (community posts/comments) — collected, used for
  app functionality.
- Data is encrypted in transit (Firebase/Firestore uses HTTPS).
- You provide a way to request data deletion (the privacy policy's contact email).
